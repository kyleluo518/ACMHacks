About Us

Who are we?

We are students of UCSC who belong to minority groups and share a common goal of expanding people's horizons. We are Nhan Nguyuen, Kyle Huo, Abby Kaur.

Why we built this?

We built this app because we believe that in today's world, it's more important than ever to be exposed to different perspectives and ideas. We recognize that people are often limited by their own biases and experiences, and may not have the opportunity or inclination to seek out new viewpoints. Our app provides a unique and accessible way for users to engage with an AI-generated persona with a specific bias, which can challenge their assumptions and broaden their understanding of the world. We believe that by fostering open-mindedness and intellectual curiosity, we can help people become more informed, empathetic, and engaged members of society.

A website made by members of the minority FOR the minority. Spreading encouragement through successes and opportunities.
